Dear Students,

NYFA LA Counseling Services is offering an anonymous assessment for stress and depression, and providing options for support. This email will provide you with more information, how it works, and how you can participate.

WHAT IS IT?

  • NYFA Cares4u-LA is a free,anonymous, online screening for stress and depression
  • Completing the screening is the first step toward reaching out a NYFA Counselor(anonymously) through a Cares4U portal. You will remain anonymous unless you decide to tell the Counselor who you are after your initial contact.

START ASSESSMENT

WHAT HAPPENS WHEN I SIGN UP?

  • After you create a unique username and password, you can take the anonymous survey which should take about 10 minutes.
  • You will be contacted via email by a NYFA Counselor about the results of your survey
  • Your identity remains private unless you decide to reveal it to your Counselor as communication goes through the Cares4U system.

WHO SHOULD SIGN UP AND TAKE THE SURVEY?

  • Any current NYFA Student can complete the assessment. It's not mandatory.
  • If you feel intense stress, worry, or hopelessness, we should like to encourage you to consider the assessment.
  • NYFA Cares4U-LA links are in the email, and will soon be available on the Student Hub and flyers around campus. You will also be receiving periodic emails reminding you of the support available.

Even if your feeling fine now, you can complete the assessment and get connected to information and use it later if you need it
